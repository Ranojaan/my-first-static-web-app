Philosophical Reflections

Existentialism

Existentialism is a philosophical perspective that emphasizes individual existence, freedom, and choice. It posits that human beings are responsible for giving meaning to their own lives and that life's inherent meaninglessness can be overcome through personal authenticity and action.

Moral Philosophy

Moral philosophy explores questions of right and wrong, good and evil, and ethical decision-making. It delves into various ethical theories, such as consequentialism, deontology, and virtue ethics, in an attempt to understand the nature of moral judgments and principles.

Metaphysics

Metaphysics is the branch of philosophy that deals with the fundamental nature of reality, including the nature of existence, time, space, causality, and the relationship between mind and body. It explores questions about the ultimate nature of reality and our place within it.
